About

Senior Lecturer in Ocean Observation at the University of Edinburgh. Principal Investigator of the FeME Network Plus project, Deputy Director for Training at SENSE, and Co-investigator at IDCORE. Affiliated with the Alan Turing Institute and University of Granada.

Research

Research interests include remote sensing of coastal environments, marine renewable energy, satellite data applications, and machine learning for environmental monitoring.
